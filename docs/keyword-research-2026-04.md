## Search-intent clusters

### 1. Results day and deadlines

Primary intents: `dse 放榜 2026`, `dse 放榜日期`, `jupas 放榜`, `jupas main round 2026`, `jupas clearing`.

Target page: `/blog/dse-results-day-2026-guide/`

Required content: July 15 results date; July 16–18 allocated modification window; August 5 Main Round; August 6 5pm acceptance-fee deadline; August 25 Clearing; direct official links and a visible last-verified date.

### 2. Modification of programme choices

Primary intents: `jupas 改選 2026`, `jupas 改選時間`, `jupas 改選 48 小時`, `jupas 改選 費用`, `jupas 加課程`.

Target page: `/blog/jupas-change-of-choices-guide/`

Required content: allocated 48-hour period, one free submission, up to five new programme choices, HK$250 resubmission, one-hour resubmission session, July 18 1:15pm final resubmission deadline. Avoid claiming Clearing admits at lower scores.

### 3. Programme priority and Band A

Primary intents: `jupas band a`, `jupas 排位`, `jupas 志願次序`, `jupas actual priority`, `jupas iteration process`.

Target page: `/blog/jupas-band-a-strategy/`

Required content: institutions see only Bands; actual priority remains confidential; iteration returns the highest actual-priority programme the applicant can obtain. Recommend genuine preference order, eligibility checks and programme-specific selection criteria. Do not use invented labels such as A4–A20 or imply earlier choices consume later chances.

### 4. Scoring formulas

Primary intents: `jupas 計分`, `jupas calculator`, `best 5 計分`, `4c+2x`, `hku jupas score calculator`, `dse 分數換算`.

Target page: `/blog/jupas-scoring-methods-explained/`

Required content: no universal conversion; course-level subject selection and weighting; dated official examples such as HKU's calculator; a verification workflow. Avoid generic tables assigning one formula or tendency to an entire university.

### 5. Historical admission scores

Primary intents: `jupas median`, `jupas lq`, `jupas 收生分數`, `jupas admission score`, `dse 分數 夠唔夠`.

Target page: `/blog/jupas-admission-scores-2026/`

Required content: statistical definitions, cohort/data-year caveats, formula comparability and direct official sources. Never describe median as safe, UQ as guaranteed, or publish unsourced universal programme ranges.

### 6. University interviews

Primary intents: `大學面試`, `大學面試問題`, `mmi 面試`, `醫科面試`, `jupas interview`.

Target page: `/blog/university-interview-preparation/`

Required content: framework-based practice, course-specific official arrangement checks and honest product scope (1,232 prompts across 14 faculty groups). Do not imply practice questions reproduce an institution's live interview.
